World Leaders

Characterization

These are people who either control the world and its top countries or will at some point in the future. They are the most powerful people.

Quote

"I lead the worlds greatest country and am one of the most respected people. I work tirelessly to better my country and the universe"

Who they are

They are people who control the world and universe. They run countries. They make the laws and enforce the laws. They have titles like King, Queen, President, and Senator.

Aspirations

The goals of folks like this are to have as much power and control as they can. A lot of the time they are focused on making the world a better place.

Attitude

They want to be able to relax from the painstaking work that is ruling. They have some of the most stressful jobs on the planet.

Behavior

They are people who need to be able to relax and unwind. They are looking for somewhere secure that they don't have to worry about being asssassinated, but can still do their duties when they need to.
