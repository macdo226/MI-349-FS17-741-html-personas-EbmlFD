The Societal Elite

Characterization

These are the wealthy people who have inherited and earned their money and are at the top of society. These are the 1 percent, no one is like them.

Quote

"I made my money the ol' fashion way. I inherited my estate from my mommy and pappy. They taught me everything I know which is to have fun!"

Who they are

They are people who make a lot of money but don't have to do a lot of work. They are usually older than 45 and white. job titles would include things like CEO or CFO.

Aspirations

The goals of folks like this are to make as much money as they can, so they can live a fun and exciting lifestyle while not having to do much work.

Attitude

They would want to be able to do what they want where they want and when they want. They dont want a bunch of rules, they want to not hjave to worry about other folks.

Behavior

They are people who are relaxed and fun to be around. they dont worry about money on a day to day basis because they have so much of it why even bother worrying.
